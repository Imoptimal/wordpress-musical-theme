Our Production Projects

Plan

This section was intended to display some of the production projects that this musical group has done for other performers – in other words their production portfolio (in the areas of song writing, song production, and music video production).

The intent was to further promote their production business side that was planned to become their primary source of interest and income in the future (after their 5 minutes of fame have passed).

Focal Point

I’ve decided to strip this page of the animations and sidebar content visible on other pages, in order to fully bring the attention of website visitors to the production projects content.

Goal

The goal was to remove all other distractions that would make potential clients click and go to another page, while providing them with a clear means to get in touch with the production crew.
